Mythical Combat Lite

Welcome To Mythical Combat Lite

What is MCL:

MCL is a Third Person combat game made by STG Studios in Unreal Engine 5 and it's not a full game so you only spawn in an area with a robot to fight with. Then you go to the gate and it opens and you enter the second level with more robots spawning. Then you go to the last level which is a small area and you can punch the robots into the void.

Controls:
WASD – Move
Right/Left Click – Punch

Minimum Requirements:
The world isn't large so the minimum requirements are similar to Unreal Engine 5 defaults.

Operating System: Windows 10 64-bit (version 1703 or later)
Processor: Quad-core Intel or AMD processor, 2.5 GHz or faster
Memory: 8 GB RAM
Graphics Card: DirectX 11 or 12 compatible graphics card
DirectX Runtime: DirectX End-User Runtimes (June 2010)
