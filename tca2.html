Welcome to The Cowboy Adventure

What is The Cowboy Adventure?

Saddle up and get ready for a fun, cartoonish journey in The Cowboy Adventure! Complete missions, explore quirky environments, and ride your trusty horse through a playful Wild West world.

This game is inspired by The Cowboy Adventure™ Alpha, keeping some of the things you loved — like the main menu music and the signature font style — while adding new adventures and fun missions. Don’t worry, the Alpha version is still available to download if you want a taste of the original!

Controls

Getting around the Wild West is easy:

W / A / S / D – Move Forward, Backward, Left, Right

Space – Jump over obstacles

Esc / P – Open the pause menu

E – Mount a horse and ride

Hop on your horse, explore, and enjoy the adventure!
